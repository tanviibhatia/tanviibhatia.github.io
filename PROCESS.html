Pre-Story: What Occurred

    Dad becomes absent
    Mom creates plan to start fire and burn down house to collect insurance money
    How did the fire start
    • Discarded lit cigarette in trash can
    • Fire starting items to be bought
    • 9 Year old used magnifying glass to burn bugs
    • Casette player playing burn the house down in edgy teen's room
    • Candle catches on fire

Game Opening Narration

    Introduces the characters
    Journal opens: Provides background, context, and goal

Story Concept

    You play as Tom Changestone, an unsirance investigator
    You must investigate a burnt down house to determine the cause of the fire
    You have a magical ability which allows you to switch between two different time zones
    • The current time (post-fire)
    • The past time(pre-fire)

Lighting

    Both sections take place at night
    Embers present in the burned house
    Flickering lights in the hallway going to the boys room
    Complete darkness in the boys room outside of some faint light sources (such as the moon or a candle to convey edginess/unease)
    Dim the lighting in the parents room to convey moral ambiguity (did they, or did they not burn down the house on purpose)

Sound Design

    Ambient Music
    Casette Tape playing Burning the House Down by the Talking Heads
    Sound Effects: Footsteps, Scribbling Sound for Clue Inspection, Answer Machine Voiceovers, Page Turning fro Reading Jounral, Intro Voice Over

Items to Model

Room Switching

    Tom can switch between the present and the past
    The present is a burnt down house
    The past is minutes before the fire
    Key to finding clues: See the cause and effect of certain objects
    Key to traversal: Certain areas will only be accessible in one time zone

Clue Discovery

    Clues will be placed around the house to be inspected
    • E.g. a diary, unpaid bills, messages on an answering machine
    Inspecting these clues will add a journal entry to your notes containing an image and a short description
    These notes will aid you in your final report

Red Herring Clues

    Mom Sequence
    • Unpaid bills in the office
    • Insurance claim form on kitchen counter
    • Answering machine in living room has call from financial advisor about problems
    • DEBUNK: Shopping list showing list of fire items to buy that haven’t been bought yet: stuck on the fridge
    Edgy Teen Sequence
    • Answering machine in living room has call from principal about son’s behavior
    • Diary with a manifesto on how he hates school and society in bedroom (post fire)
    • Cassette player playing song “Burning down the House” by the Talking Heads in bedroom (pre fire)
    • DEBUNK: Left for road-trip 3 weeks before fire
    Miscellaneous 1
    • Candle stick on dining room table
    • DEBUNK: Candle stick remains there post fire
    Miscellaneous 2
    • Ashtray&Cigarette in metal trash can in kitchen
    • DEBUNK: Cigarette remains there post fire

Real Clues

    Magnifying glass on child’s desk in pre-fire
    Small burn marks on their desk and floor in pre-fire house
    Books about bugs
    Post-fire room, the desk is completely reduced to ash, only magnifying glass left

Report Filling

    After you have searched the house, you must file a report to your company
    • Desribe the mean, motive, and perpetrator
    This report will decide if the family of the house receives insurance money
    The report will be multiple choice
    • Options will change depending on which clues you discovered
